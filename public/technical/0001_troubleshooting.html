The Art of Troubleshooting

July 16, 2026

I taught a class on troubleshooting for my job. I thought the material was valuable so I am posting some of it here to share.

These next sections go over general troubleshooting philosophy. You will be able to apply these ideas to anything.

The Rules

  • Number 1: Know the system and the process.
  • Number 2: Never make assumptions.

The Steps

  • Number 1: Identify the problem.
  • Number 2: Specify what the problem is.
  • Number 3: Make a list of possible failures.
  • Number 4: Eliminate each possible failure one by one.
  • Number 5: Identify the failure.
  • Number 6: Analyze and fix the failure.
  • Number 7: Retest the requirements.

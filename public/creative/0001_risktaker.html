Risk Taker - a rough draft

July 16, 2026

It's ok
To be scared
To be anxious
To feel like the world
Should be exactly as you
See it
One day
You’ll decide to change the lens
Because of a change in scenery
Or failure
I think its funny how
“one day you might stand
on paradise, not move
and soon be drowning”
Not because it was funny at the time
But because there is a period of time
Between one lens and the next
Where everything is so disorienting,
Its funny looking-
Like you

Sometimes it's ok to take a risk
I hope you take it
